# Community

![Speech bubbles converging on a central narrative card](/images/heroes/community.png){.page-hero}

NDD is usable today and still evolving.

The method is working:

- domains
- narratives
- scenes
- moments
- command, query, react, and experience moments
- rules and examples
- should statements
- data completeness as a review principle
- visual and document review

The Auto product adds the workflow depth needed for AI-assisted implementation.

That is the honest state.

## Join early

- use NDD on real app ideas
- challenge the language
- help shape the taxonomy
- discuss AI-assisted development beyond prompt soup
- compare NDD with BDD, DDD, EventStorming, and spec-driven development

## Build with Auto

Auto is how NDD ships as a product. It applies NDD to your prompt, turns it into a reviewable narrative, and supports AI-assisted implementation inside the product workflow.

**[Open Auto](https://on.auto)**: the productized NDD workflow with visual review, document review, collaboration, and build support.
